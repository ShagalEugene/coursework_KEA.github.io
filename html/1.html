Game Rules

A track with a car and a button to start will appear. When you press the button, the car will begin moving. You need to calculate how far the car has moved. You have 3 attempts to answer. The closer the answer is to the correct answer, the more points you will earn.

To unlock the next level, you need to score 100 points.

If you want to start the game, please press and hold Enter
