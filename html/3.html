Game Rules

After the launch, one of three roads is randomly generated. Two seconds after clicking the "Start Game" button, a time input field will appear. The closer your car gets to the edge of the road, the more points you earn. If your car falls off the road, no points will be added to your final score. To complete a level, you must earn 100 points.

If you want to start the game, please press Enter
